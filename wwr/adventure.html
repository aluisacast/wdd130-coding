This is a fun, familiar, exciting and very moderate risk, a beautiful setting touring the lower part of the Micos waterfalls.
The route is 10 kilometers and class II. The rainy season is the ideal time to do this walk.

Tampeon River

It runs in the middle of a great canyon made of limestone with turquoise blue waters, an authentic wonder of the Huasteca Potosina. This 18 km route is an exciting challenge since the river has class III rapids formed by large rocks and the abundant flow of water from the river.
A hidden beauty of the Tampeon river is a beautiful cavern flooded with crystalline water and navy blue tones, called Cueva del Agua.
